Illustration

The Bestiary

The Bestiary is a personal illustration project that I took on in order to further my drawing abilities in general as well as my ability to draw animals and strange creatures. I am a huge fan of fantasy, and drawing such creatures as these is something I've always wished I could do better, and now I have that skill — and a cool poster.

After finishing the illustrations, I decided that I wanted to do something with the design that could get it out into the world and give people a chance to see it. Therefore, I designed a deck of cards using a number of my creatures, giving them names and descriptions and creating a world for them to exist in.

(P.S. If you want to support my work and get a deck of cards, visit https://www.makeplayingcards.com/sell/sparhawk-creative)
