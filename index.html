About Me

Cooper Smith

For me, art is a lifestyle. I live and breathe graphite and pixels. Not really, that would hurt — but I do draw a lot, digitally and traditionally. As hobbies, I enjoy wildlife photography and hiking, reading, as well as playing guitar.

I began studying design in 2016 at Eastern Washington University, and in 2020 I will graduate with a Bachelor of Design.

My passion is in illustration and layout design, but I have some experience in motion, photography, web and UX.
